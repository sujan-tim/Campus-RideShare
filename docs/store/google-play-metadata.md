RUride brings campus ride booking and Rutgers transit tracking into one mobile app.

Use RUride to:

- Book campus rides between Rutgers locations
- See live driver progress and street-based trip navigation
- Track Rutgers buses on a live map
- View bus stops, route details, and ETA predictions
- Manage payment methods for rides
- Review recent trips and rider history

RUride is designed to make campus transportation easier to understand and faster to use.

Features include:

- Ride booking flow for campus trips
- Live trip maps for riders and drivers
- Real-time Rutgers bus tracking experience
- Route lines, stop lists, and arrival estimates
- Mobile-focused checkout and saved payment methods
- Profile and ride history management

Location access helps power map positioning, trip tracking, and transit tools while the app is in use.

Important:

- Live ride, payment, and transit experiences depend on the deployed backend and connected services.
- Availability of specific features can vary by release environment.

RUride is built for students who want one place for campus mobility.
